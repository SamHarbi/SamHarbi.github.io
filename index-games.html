Bubble Fairy

A 2D Arcade game made in 3 days
Summary

I joined the 84th Mini Jam that ran over a course of three days last summer. This online game jam had the goal of making a game that roughly fit the theme of bubbles but had to follow the limitation of you are your own ammo. I made a arcade styled dish cleaning minigame where the goal was to stop any dirty dishes from getting past you- but the catch is that you play as a swarm of soap bubbles that breaks down everytime a dish was cleaned. Made in Unity with all visual assets made by me within the time frame

DUCS Squad Builder

24hrs DUCS Hackathon Winning game chosen by Unity Technologies
Summary

I worked as part of a team to create a Banana-Duck themed game in 24 hours during the in-person 2022 Quackathon organised by The Dundee Computing Society, our game ended up being selected by Unity Technologies (One of multiple sponsors at the event) as the winning team.
